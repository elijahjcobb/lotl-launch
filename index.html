The Lesson of the Lark

by Jeffrey Cobb, based on the book by Laura Knight Cobb

PREMIERING FRIDAY, DECEMBER 4, 7:00PM

a collaborative project featuring the Northwestern Michigan College Choirs, Music Faculty, Visual Communications Students, and Audio Tech Students

"The Lesson of the Lark" – a new concert work celebrating the 100th Anniversary of the ratification of Women’s Voting Rights. This piece, which is meant to uplift and unify us during these challenging times, is a multi-movement work composed for narrator, soprano and mezzo-soprano soloists, chorus, and jazz orchestra. The work takes its inspiration from the language of Jazz, including elements of Swing, Blues, Bossa Nova, Gospel, Salsa, and improvisation.
